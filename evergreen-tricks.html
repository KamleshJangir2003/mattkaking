Trick 1 - Final Ank Formula

Add all 3 digits of any Panna. The last digit of the sum is the Final Ank.

Example: Panna = 2,2,7 → 2+2+7 = 11 → Final Ank = 1

Example: Panna = 4,5,9 → 4+5+9 = 18 → Final Ank = 8

Trick 2 - Jodi from Open Close

Combine Open Ank + Close Ank to form Jodi.

Open = 1, Close = 6 → Jodi = 16, 61

Open = 3, Close = 8 → Jodi = 38, 83
